A climb to La Cumbre with Adrenalina

La Paz - Bolivia

July-2018

My first time with the Club Adrenalina 4x4 Bolivia, this time we adventured to the start point of "La Cumbre". I was in great company, that means with my one and only Carola and her dad Ruddy, who I must say it is a master on the wheel. So we took off from ·Las Cholas", we had some introductions there while the staff of the club was setting details on the journey of the day. We crossed the city to get on the road. We were able to see wonderful landscape in La Paz.

Ilegal Radio Station Operative

La Paz - Bolivia

October-2018

One of the competence of ATT is to regulate telecomunications and so they also manage the electric spectrum of frequencies, this means they make sure nothing ilegal happens to them. Ilegal radio Stations is a growing problem, therefore ATT puts together operatives in order to stop them. This is one of the many operatives I was part of, my job being record video and take photographs of all the actions taken place at site.
